## Dinner
**6.00pm - 10.00pm** <br />
**Air Force Museum of New Zealand, 45 Harvard Avenue, Wigram, Christchurch**<br />

The Thursday night conference dinner will be held at the Air Force Museum. We look forward to seeing you there!

Buses will depart Te Pae conference centre at 5.15pm.

The dinner is open to partners. Please register them as an additional attendee, and tick just the "partner ticket" option so we can ensure everyone is seated and catered for.

Drinks will be served on arrival and all have the opportunity to view the Museum before sitting down for dinner.

Buses will return guests to Te Pae after dinner.
