### Jetex Flight Simulator
Friday 27th March - from 5pm

*$110 per person*

1A Hawthornden Road, Avonhead, Christchurch

Experience an exact replica of the world's best-selling aircraft in aviation history - the Boeing 737. It is a CAA certified 'fixed base' flight training device and is used by commercial pilots, as a lead-in to full flight simulator (airline) training.  If you haven't flown before, no problem because you will be guided by a fully qualified simulator instructor. The high-tech instructor station (behind the pilots) allows us to set almost any scenario. You could be flying in Cairo, New York, London or Sydney. ### Christchurch Tramway Restaurant
Friday 27 March, 5pm or 7.30pm.

- *5.00pm (3 course) $125pp*
- *7.30pm (4 course) $149pp*

Departs from 111 Worcester St

The Tramway Restaurant offers curated seasonal dining combined with evening sightseeing in central Christchurch for a truly unique experience!

Seats are limited, so please book for this early.

Includes welcome drink, starter (7.30pm only), entree (chef's choice), main (choice of 3) and dessert (choice of 3) with tea or coffee. Beverages additional and can be purchased while dining. Please advise dietary requirements on booking.

Bookings required before 20th February.

[https://www.christchurchattractions.nz/christchurch-tramway-restaurant/](https://www.christchurchattractions.nz/christchurch-tramway-restaurant/)
